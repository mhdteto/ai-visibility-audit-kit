# AI Visibility Audit Checklist

## Entity clarity

- [ ] Brand name is consistent across public profiles.
- [ ] Website clearly states services, market and location.
- [ ] About page explains the person or company behind the brand.
- [ ] Service pages use plain and specific language.

## Source consistency

- [ ] LinkedIn, website and directories use aligned descriptions.
- [ ] Contact information is consistent.
- [ ] Location and market are clear.
- [ ] Old or conflicting descriptions are removed.

## Citation readiness

- [ ] Important pages have clear headings.
- [ ] FAQ sections answer buyer questions directly.
- [ ] Claims are supported by examples, case studies or testimonials.
- [ ] Pages are easy to quote and summarize.

## AI answer testing

- [ ] Branded prompts tested.
- [ ] Category prompts tested.
- [ ] Local intent prompts tested.
- [ ] Comparison prompts tested.
- [ ] Recommendation prompts tested.

## Reputation signals

- [ ] Public testimonials exist.
- [ ] Case studies are available.
- [ ] Third-party mentions are documented.
- [ ] Reviews or references support the positioning.

## Technical basics

- [ ] Website is crawlable.
- [ ] Metadata is clear.
- [ ] Internal links connect related pages.
- [ ] `llms.txt` is available if relevant.
