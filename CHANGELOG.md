### Purpose

This first version provides a practical structure to evaluate how a brand appears across AI answer engines such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude and Google AI Overviews.

The goal is to help consultants, SMBs and B2B teams measure:

- Entity clarity.
- Source consistency.
- AI answer presence.
- Citation readiness.
- Structured content.
- Reputation signals.

### Notes

This project does not promise guaranteed visibility, rankings or citations in AI-generated answers.

It provides a practical audit framework to identify visibility gaps and prioritize improvements.
