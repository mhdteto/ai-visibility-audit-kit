# Roadmap

## Current version

v1.0 - Initial public framework for AI visibility audits.

## Purpose

This project provides a practical framework to evaluate how a brand appears across AI answer engines such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude and Google AI Overviews.

The goal is to help consultants, SMBs and B2B teams turn AI visibility into a measurable business review instead of a vague marketing concept.
