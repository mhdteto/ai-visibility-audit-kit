## Result

The brand was not mentioned in the first answer.

The AI system recommended competitors and generic agencies instead.

## Diagnosis

Example Company has a clear website but lacks enough third-party validation and category-level content.

The AI system understands the company when asked directly, but does not associate it strongly with broader service-category prompts such as:

- AI automation consultant.
- AI consultant in Casablanca.
- LLM visibility expert.
- GEO consultant for SMBs.

## Main issues

1. The brand is not strongly associated with category-level queries.
2. The website has limited citation-ready content.
3. Public profiles use inconsistent descriptions.
4. There are few third-party reputation signals.
5. There are no visible case studies.
6. The brand is understood in branded prompts but weak in recommendation prompts.

## Recommended actions

1. Create a dedicated service page for AI automation in Casablanca.
2. Add a clear author or entity page.
3. Publish one case study.
4. Add FAQ blocks to service pages.
5. Align LinkedIn and directory descriptions.
6. Build third-party mentions and reviews.
7. Create or improve the `llms.txt` file.
8. Add structured sections explaining services, audience and use cases.
9. Repeat the audit after 30 days.

## Priority

High.

## Expected business value

Improving these areas should make the brand easier for AI systems to understand, describe and potentially reference in relevant answers.

This does not guarantee AI recommendations, but it improves the quality and consistency of the public signals available to AI answer engines.
