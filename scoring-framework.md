# AI Visibility Scoring Framework

| Dimension | Weight | Score |
|---|---:|---:|
| Entity clarity | 20 | /20 |
| Source consistency | 20 | /20 |
| AI answer presence | 20 | /20 |
| Citation readiness | 15 | /15 |
| Structured content | 15 | /15 |
| Reputation signals | 10 | /10 |
| **Total** | **100** | **/100** |

## Scoring logic

### Entity clarity

Does the AI system understand who the brand is, what it does and where it operates?

### Source consistency

Are the website, LinkedIn, directories, profiles and public mentions aligned?

### AI answer presence

Does the brand appear in answers for relevant category and local-intent prompts?

### Citation readiness

Does the brand have clear, quotable and source-friendly pages?

### Structured content

Does the website include structured content, FAQ, service pages, author pages and clear internal linking?

### Reputation signals

Are there external reviews, testimonials, case studies, interviews, citations or third-party references?

## Interpretation

| Score | Status | Meaning |
|---:|---|---|
| 80-100 | Strong | The brand is visible, clear and supported by useful sources. |
| 60-79 | Developing | The brand is understandable but has citation or reputation gaps. |
| 40-59 | Weak | AI systems may mention the brand inconsistently or inaccurately. |
| 0-39 | At risk | The brand is unlikely to appear in category or recommendation answers. |
